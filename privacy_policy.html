Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Welcome to the Privacy Policy for RoutineTask: Repeat Alarm. This document explains how we handle your data, our advertising policies, and our target audience compliance to ensure transparency and trust in accordance with Google Play Store policies.

1. Data Safety & Collection

We prioritize your privacy and believe in data minimization. Our application operates completely locally on your device.

  • Personal Information: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, emails, contacts, or location data.
  • App Data: All alarms, schedules, and preferences you set are stored locally securely in your device's internal database. This data never leaves your device.
  • Device Permissions: We strictly only request permissions necessary for the app's core alarm functionality:
    • Post Notifications: Required to visually and audibly notify you when an alarm goes off.
    • Schedule Exact Alarms: Required to ensure alarms ring precisely at the time you configured, even in Doze mode.
    • Receive Boot Completed: Required to reschedule your active alarms seamlessly after you restart your device.
    • Read External Storage / Media Audio: Required if you choose to select a custom ringtone or audio file from your device for your alarms. This permission strictly only accesses audio files when you initiate a ringtone change.
    • Wake Lock: Required to wake up your device's screen and keep the processor running long enough to ring your scheduled alarms.
    • Vibrate: Required to utilize the device’s vibration motor alongside the audio alarm.
  • Data Sharing: Because no data leaves your device, we absolutely do not share any of your data with third parties.

2. Advertising Policy (Ads)

Our app provides a clean, uninterrupted experience.

  • Ad-Free Experience: This application does not contain any third-party advertisements.
  • No Tracking: We do not integrate with ad networks, marketing tracking SDKs, or analytics services that monitor your behavior across other apps or websites.

3. Target Audience & Children's Privacy

We are committed to protecting the privacy of all users, especially children.

  • Target Audience: Our application is a general-utility tool designed for a broad audience, primarily targeting users aged 13 and older.
  • Children Under 13: While the app is perfectly safe for anyone to use (as it lacks data collection and ads), it is not specifically targeted at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has inadvertently provided us with personal information, please contact us.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our app's features or regulatory requirements from app distribution platforms. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
